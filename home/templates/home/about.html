About Trans-Atlantic

The Trans-Atlantic Trading company is a multi-faceted company that was formed to respond to needs in the imports and exports of high demand products and commodities, provision of IT Services as well as general supplies.

Trading Department

The Trading Department addresses a gap in the marketplace both locally and overseas with respect to imports and exports. Equipped with and effective and dedicated team, we are able to supply large quantities of quality produce and products expeditiously, efficiently and at competitive prices.

Our Exports

We export a wide array of agricultural produce including but not limited to: Quality Kenyan Tea, Quality Kenyan Coffee, Pineapples, Mangoes, Avocados, Macadamia Nuts and Ghee, meat and Meat products as well as Livestock.

Our Imports

Our imports include: Cement, Sugar, Petroleum and Petroleum Products, Industrial Chemicals and Machinery, Pharmaceuticals and Medical Equipment, High Demand Processed Foods among others.
